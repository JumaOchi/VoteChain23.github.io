Why VoteChain?

Inspiration for?

This project drew inspiration from the recently concluded general elections in kenya. The process was murred with a number of problems from irregularities,Lack of trust from all players, poor transmission of results and a very expensive election. This here is a low fidelity solution that attempts to solve this problems.

  • VoteChain platform system
  • Ensuring optimal security
  • Immutable so vote is secure
  • Can be audited to confirm integrity
  • Fast and cost effective
  • Honesty and integrity at the core
